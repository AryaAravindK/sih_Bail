UNDER SECTION 437 OF THE CODE OF CRIMINAL PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE, the above named accused most respectfully submits as follows:

That the complainant police have registered a case against the accused for an alleged offence punishable under section (SECTION NUMBER)

2.   That the accused is innocent of the said offence and has got valid and tenable defense.

3.   That the accused have been falsely implicated in the alleged offence at the instance of the persons who are enimically disposed towards the accused.

4.   The accused is a peace-loving and law-abiding citizen and has no bad antecedents. Further, the alleged offence is neither punishable with death nor imprisonment for life.

5.   The accused are permanent residents and will neither abscond nor delay the proceedings.

6.   The accused is ready and willing to abide by any condition or conditions that may be imposed by this Hon'ble Court, in the event of allowing this application. Further, the accused is ready to offer solvent surety to the satisfaction of this Hon'ble court for the due appearance.

WHEREFORE, the accused prays that this Hon'ble Court be pleased to enlarge the accused on bail, in the interest of justice and equity.
