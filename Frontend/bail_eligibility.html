Further Steps

1
Hire a Lawyer

Engage a lawyer to prepare and file the bail application.

2
Draft and File the Application

Your lawyer drafts the bail application with details of the offense and reasons for seeking bail. File it in the appropriate court.

3
Court Hearing

Attend the hearing where the court reviews arguments from both the defense and prosecution.

4
Furnish Bail Bond

If bail is granted, provide the required bail bond or surety and comply with any court conditions for release.
