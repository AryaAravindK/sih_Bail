What is Bail Reckoner?

Bail Reckoner is an innovative digital tool designed to assist undertrial prisoners, legal aid providers, and judicial authorities in streamlining the bail application process. With its automated features and legal insights, the Bail Reckoner simplifies bail decisions by analyzing key legal parameters and procedural requirements, ensuring transparency, efficiency, and justice.
